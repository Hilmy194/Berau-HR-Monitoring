# PROJECT CONTEXT

## Business Background

This application is intended for HR probation monitoring.

A new employee typically has a probation period of 100 days.

The application should provide transparency for employees while allowing HR to manage the process.

The application is NOT a full HRIS.

The application only focuses on:

1. Probation Activities
2. Probation Presentation

---

## Probation Activities

Employees must complete several onboarding and probation activities.

Examples:

* Onboarding
* Employee ID collection
* Laptop collection
* Email activation
* HR orientation
* Department orientation
* Safety training
* Mid probation review

Employees only need to see progress.

HR manages all updates.

---

## Probation Presentation

At the end of probation, employees must attend a probation presentation.

Employees can see:

* Date
* Time
* Location
* Meeting Link
* Assigned Panelists
* Final Score
* Recommendation

HR manages all data.

---

## Permissions
